About Us

Development of an iot based flood and weather monitoring system is created to efficiently measure the parameters of weather change with respect to flood monitoring. It has been found that before flood occurs there are some changes in the surrounding environment like change in temperature although changes in temperature may not always be accurate because in northeastern area of the subcontinent there are drastic changes which take place very quickly in weather. another change in surrounding that can be seen as a flood indicator is the humidity level, this would help us detect about the rain. The wind speed on the other hand is a good indicator of flood and the direction of flood but our project focusses mainly on the water level of the specific area which would be calculated by our system by comparing the water level values with certain constraints and prespecified conditions and send an alert and display the information in our website. This would be a step forward in providing first hand information on details regarding flood.



Operation

working of the project: Our system works on GSM technology and is solar powered by a solar panel of ........ . Solar energy powers our battery which is capable of powering up the whole system. A solar charge controller monitors the charge usage and power delivery to other components. we have included NodeMCU ESP8266 chipset which is a wi-fi enabled microcontroller which would connect to the server cloud and send information to our website. The main component of our system is the NodeMCU and an Ultrasonic Sonar sensor which would detect the water level and send this data to NodeMCU for processing.
